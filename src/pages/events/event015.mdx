### Event Instructions

1. Do not transmit on fox frequency unless on emergency.
2. The winner should handover his rig & antenna to fox upon finding foxhole.
3. Entries to be given to Deepan vu2dpn.Police permission copy will be made 
Available. Participants are requested to register in google sheet and 
Intimate payment details in wa group of the event.
4. Carry your id cards / aadhar / driving license / ham license.
5. Park vehicles well off the road especially on major junctions/ highways. 
Do not park on narrow roads/bridges.
6. Ensure adequate fuel availability for onward and return journey.
7. The fox hole may not be on a busy road where vehicles are driven. Be prepared to walk, climb, 
Crawl.
8. Drive carefully. Two wheeler riders must wear helmet.
9. Abide by all usual safety regulations, instructions from police 
Department. Follow traffic signals and drive with in speed limits.
10. Fox decision will be final in case of any disputes.
